title: Microsoft Teams Overview
---

Microsoft Teams is a free service (limited functionality) and can also be part of the Office 365 Platform, which requires a subscription to Office 365 Business Essentials or Office 365 Business Premium. The activities in the Microsoft Teams integration can be used to receive user messages to the Resolve Teams Bot and trigger workflows to resolve issues, as well as manage teams and channels in Teams.
