title: Enabling a Form
---
You must enable a Form to make it available in the [Self Service Portal](../../Home-Page/Self-Service-Portal/Introduction-to-Self-Service-Portal.mdx).

An **enabled** form is visible in the [Self Service Portal](../../Home-Page/Self-Service-Portal/Introduction-to-Self-Service-Portal.mdx) to all users with permission to see it. Forms that are not enabled are not visible within the Self Service Portal.

Take these steps to enable a form:

1. Open the form in the Self Service Designer.
2. In the **Control Panel**, check the **Enabled** box.
3. Optionally, check **Confirm before form submission** to require users to confirm a submission when they click the Submit button in the Portal.
