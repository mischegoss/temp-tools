## Permissions

The Activity Designer is only available to users with the **Administrator** role or the **Developer** role. The **Developer** role has all the rights of the **Workflow Editor** role with the addition of being able to see the **Activity Designer**.

## Navigation

Log in to your VAR::PRODUCT_FULL tenant and from the navigation menu in the the **Builder** section click **Activity Designer**. 

The first time you access the Activity Designer, you will be presented with a guide that outlines the usage of the tool. It will be presented every time you enter the Activity Designer unless the **Do not show this message again** box is checked. Click **OK** to close the window.
