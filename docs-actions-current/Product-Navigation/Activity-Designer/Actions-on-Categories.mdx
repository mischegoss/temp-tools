New categories can be created, and existing categories can be deleted or edited.

## Creating a Category

For more information about categories, see the [Starting a New Activity](./New-Activity-Module.mdx) section.

Take these steps to create a new category:

1. Open the Activity Designer.
2. In the left navigation bar, click **+ New Category**.
3. In the **New Category** dialog box that appears, enter the category name.

You can also create a new category from the New Activity Modal.

## Creating a Subcategory

Subcategories are organized as folders within existing folders. Follow these steps after creating a category:

1. Open the Activity Designer.
2. In the left navigation bar, click **+ New Category**.
3. In the **Create Category** dialog box: 
   1. Enter the *Category Name.*
   2. In *Create as a sub-folder of:* select the name of the parent folder.

## Renaming a Category

Take these steps to rename a category:

1. Open the Activity Designer.
2. In the left navigation bar, hover your mouse over the category label.
3. Click the **Pencil** icon.

The **Rename Category** modal will open, where you can change the category name to another unique name.

## Deleting a Category

Only empty categories can be deleted. To delete a category that has activities in it, use the **Edit Metadata** option in the activity to move the activity to another category until the desired category is empty.

Take these steps to rename a category:

1. Open the Activity Designer.
2. In the left navigation bar, hover your mouse over the category label.
3. Click the trash can icon.
