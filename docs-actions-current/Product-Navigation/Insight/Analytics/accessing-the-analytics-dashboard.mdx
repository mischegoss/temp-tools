Before you can access the **Analytics** dashboard, ensure that your VAR::PRODUCT_FULL user account is [assigned one of the roles](./granting-access-to-the-analytics-dashboard.mdx) that are allowed to view it.

To access the Analytics Dashboard, go to the **Main Menu** and under **Insight**, click **Analytics**.
