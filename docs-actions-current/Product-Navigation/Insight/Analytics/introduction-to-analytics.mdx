title: Introduction to Analytics
---

The Analytics dashboard provides metrics that help you track whether your company or organization is meeting its automation objectives in a visually rich way. It also makes it easy to communicate, internally or externally, the value that Actions brings.

Through metrics such as cost reduction and FTE hours saved, you can make more informed decisions and steer the internal development toward the workflows that increase the ROI the most.
