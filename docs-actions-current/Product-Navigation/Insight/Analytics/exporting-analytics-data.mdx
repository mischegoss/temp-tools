title: Exporting Analytics Data
---

You can export the Analytics Dashboard either in PDF or XLS file format:

* The PDF export gives you the data in the graphical format that you see on the Analytics Dashboard.
* The XLS export gives you the data in numerical format that you can more easily include in your reports or analyses.

Take the following steps to export the Analytics Dashboard data:

1. Click the **Export** button in the top-right corner.
2. Select the export format.
3. Save the download on your computer.
