title: Editing and Deleting a Folder
---

Follow these instructions to delete or change the name of a folder.

1. Within the Self Service Designer, hover your mouse over the folder name.  
2. Click **Edit** (pencil icon) to change the folder name.  
   :::note
   Folder names must be unique across all folders.
   :::
3. Click **Delete** (trash can icon) to delete the folder.  
   :::note CAUTION
   Deleting a folder will also delete all forms in that folder. If you do not want to delete the forms, move them before deletion.
   :::
