title: Moving a Form
---

It is possible to change the folder where a form resides without re-saving the Form as a new version (Save As). Moving a form is helpful if you want to delete a folder and keep the forms in that folder.

1. Open the Form in the Self Service Designer.
2. Click the **three-dot menu** in the Form tab and select **Edit**.  
3. Within the **Folder** dropdown, select the new folder location. 
4. Click **Save** to move the Form into the new folder.
