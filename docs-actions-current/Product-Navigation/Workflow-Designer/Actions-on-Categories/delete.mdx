To delete a category:

1. Open the left drawer menu and hover over the name of the category you want to delete.
2. Click the **trash can** icon that appears. 
3. Confirm that you want to delete the category and **ALL** the activities within it.

:::note

If activities are assigned to a worfklow, you will not be able to delete the category.

:::

For more information on deleting individual activities, see [Deleting Activities](../Actions-on-Activities/delete.mdx).

### Delete a Category and Maintain Activities

If you want to delete a category but keep its activities:

1. Open each activity and use the *Edit Metadata* option to move it to a different category.
2. Once all activities have been reassigned, follow the steps in [Deleting a Category](#deleting-a-category) above. 

:::warning

Deleted activities cannot be restored.

:::
