title: Paste
---
The **Paste** action lets you quickly move or duplicate activities within the same workflow or across different workflows.

To paste an activity:
1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the left side of the activity you want to move or copy, then select:
   1. **Cut** to move the activity, or
   2. **Copy** to create a duplicate.
2. Go to the desired location and choose one of the following methods to paste:
   1. Click the **three-dot** menu of the activity above your target location and select **Paste**.
   2. Click the **node** where ou want to insert the activity. In the placeholder, that appears, click **Paste**.
