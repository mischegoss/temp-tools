title: Disable
---
By default, all activities in a workflow are **enabled**, meaning they will run during workflow execution. While designing a workflow, you may want to disable specific activities to test portions of the workflow.

To disable and enable an activity:

1. Locate the **toggle** on the right side of the activity.
2. To **disable** the activity, move the toggle to the **left**.
    The toggle appears gray, indicating that the activity is disabled.
3. To **enable** the activity, move the toggle to the **right**. 
    The toggle appears blue, and the activity becomes active.
