title: Introduction to Workflows and Templates
---

The **Workflows** section under **Repositories** provides facilities to manage both your workflows and your templates. If you only use prepared workflows, then this section will provide a catalog of available workflows. You will probably not need the Templates section. If however, you prepare or modify workflows, this section will show you how to catalog your workflows into folders. It will also show you how to catalog your templates.
