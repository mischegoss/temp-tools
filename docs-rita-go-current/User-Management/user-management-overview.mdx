The User Management dashboard allows you to assign roles, invite new users, and delete users.

## The User Management Dashboard
To see all users and perform actions on their accounts:
1. Navigate to your username in the bottom right corner of the dashboard and click **Settings**.
2. In the left panel, click **Users**.
3. Toggle between existing **Users** and **Invite Pending** to see users who have active invitations. 

### Add Additional Users
To add more users:
1. Click **+Invite Users** in the top right. 
2. In the *Invite Users* window, enter the emails of users. 
3. Click the **Invite Users** button.
