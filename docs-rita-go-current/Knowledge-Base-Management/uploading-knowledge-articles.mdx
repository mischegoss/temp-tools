Click the **+ Add Articles** button on the **Knowledge Articles Dashboard** to open the file finder on your device.

Supported file types include **.pdf, .csv, .txt,** and **.doc**.

After selecting your files, you can monitor their ingestion progress in the **Status** column on the dashboard.
A pop-up dialog will appear once the ingestion process is complete.
