If your instance of Rita Go contains inaccurate or outdated information, you will need to edit your knowledge articles manually. To do this: 

1. Navigate to the **Knowledge Articles** dashboard.
2. Click the **three dots** to the right of the article name.
3. Click **Download**.
4. Once successfully downloaded, click **Delete**.
5. Make edits to your document. 
6. [Reupload the knowledge article](./uploading-knowledge-articles).
