Once you've configured Confluence to sync with Rita Go, you can edit your authentication settings or update your synced spaces.

### Change Authentication Settings

To update your URL, user email, or API token:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Connection Sources**.
2. Click the **Manage** button on the Confluence tile.
3. Click the **three dots** and select **Edit** to the right of Confluence Configuration.
4. Update the settings.
5. Click **Save**.

### Update Synced spaces

To add, delete, or modify synced spaces in your Confluence instance:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Connection Sources**.
2. Click the **Manage** button on the Confluence tile.
3. Under *Which spaces would you like to sync from?*, add or delete spaces from your instance.
4. Click the **Sync** button.
