Once you have a working chat and validated articles, you can invite your team to join you.

1. On the dashboard, click **+Invite Users** in the bottom right corner.
2. In the *Invite Users* window, enter the email addresses of users. 
3. Click the **Invite Users** button.

For more information about users, see [User Management Overview](../User-Management/user-management-overview).
