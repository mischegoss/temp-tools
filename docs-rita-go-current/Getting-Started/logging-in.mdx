To log in in to **RITA Go**, navigate to your instance URL. 

In the **Sign In** module, enter your *email* and *password*, then click **Sign In**.

![Rita Go Login](img/rita-go-login.png)

If you do not have an existing account, you must either be invited to an instance by a colleague or sign up for [RITA Go Early Access](https://resolve.io/rita-go-early-access). Signing up will make you the administrator of the instance.

:::note

Your email may only be associated with one Rita Go instance. If you sign up as an administrator, note that you cannot be invited to another instance.

:::
